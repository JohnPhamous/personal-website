Projects

A collection of what I do for fun. I use GitHub to showcase my code for these projects.

Citrus Hack 2018

Citrus Hack is a 24 hour hackathon hosted at UC Riverside. I designed the website with the help of Citrus Hack's marketing team. I also implemented a feature where the color scheme and background change according to the time of day.

Alcee Insurance

Alcee Insurance is a truck insurance company located in Glendale, California. I developed a static desktop website for them. Users can learn about services offered, find contact information, and submit a form to get an insurance quote.

Flooferbot

An automated Twitter client that takes images of floofy dog breeds from the Dog API. Posts on a 30 minute interval. Good bot.

B-ify
